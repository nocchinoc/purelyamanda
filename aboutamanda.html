Meet Amanda...

Hi! I'm Amanda. Welcome to my personal website! A one-stop-shop where you can learn a little more about me and all my ventures. This particular page shares a little more about my background. Feel free to click on any of my other tabs above regarding my current projects.

As a child, I moved with my family often. My interests and talents are a collection of my experiences gained over time. I grew to love change and found excitement in trying new things and meeting new people. From a young age, I developed a steady involvement in the performing arts. I grew quite comfortable being in front of a crowd. In fact, I thrived.

Today, you can find me any given day of the week front and center whether leading a large studio yoga class, singing on stage at local events or making people laugh at my local improv theater. But my ability to juggle all these passions of mine didn't happen overnight...

I earned my Education degree at the University of Florida and I gave teaching a good go but found in my heart, a school environment just wasn't my true calling. So I sought out travel to help me figure out what to do next in my life. I spent time living in both Italy and South Korea, earning an income as an English teacher.

What I didn’t expect was the amount of growth and self-discovery that I found along the way. Especially my desire to earn my yoga teaching certification shortly after in San Francisco. You can learn more about my yoga career and offerings HERE. I attribute my confidence to pursue all of my passions today from my time spent living abroad, including my dedication to promoting healthy eating and positive body image awareness for all women. You can check out a very heartfelt memoir about this HERE >insert link to BadYogi article<.

Recently, I hatched a side passion-project: plant-based, whole foods meals in Mason jars I aptly call Pure Jars. You can learn more about these little bundles of nourishment HERE.

There’s no way of knowing what I will get into next! While my wanderlust always calls for me, I'm appreciating every day having the privilege of living a life doing exactly what I love. Currently holding down the fort in St. Petersburg, FL, I invite you to reach out for any inquires regarding yoga, Pure Jars or singing for hire.
